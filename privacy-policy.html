PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: March 31, 2026

1. Overview

Poke-A-Bird ("the Game") is a bird-collecting mobile game developed by Benjamin Tonelli ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information, if any, is collected when you use the Game, and how we handle it.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Poke-A-Bird is designed to be enjoyed without collecting personal information.

2. Information We Collect

Poke-A-Bird does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not collect device identifiers or advertising IDs.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services.
  • All game progress and data is stored locally on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

The Game is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect certain data as part of the App Store platform and in-app purchase infrastructure. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on their data practices.

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social media SDKs.

4. Children's Privacy

Poke-A-Bird is appropriate for all ages. Because we do not collect any personal information from any users, no special provisions are required for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children or any other users.

5. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no user data stored on our servers to secure. Your game data remains on your device and is subject to your device's own security measures.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
